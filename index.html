The most innovative personal care products of 2023

In 2023, some of the most innovative personal care products were those that use sustainable and eco-friendly materials, such as products packaged in glass or biodegradable materials. Additionally, there was a growing trend towards personal care products that use natural and organic ingredients, and products that cater to specific skin concerns such as hyperpigmentation and sensitive skin. Some other innovations include personal care products that use new technology, such as virtual try-on features or smart mirrors, to enhance the user experience. Another trend was the incorporation of CBD oil in personal care products such as lotions, serums and facial oils, as it is known to have anti-inflammatory properties.
